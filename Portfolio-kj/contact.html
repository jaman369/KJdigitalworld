FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the questions I hear most often from potential clients.

How long does it take to see SEO results?

+

SEO is a long-term strategy. While some improvements can be seen within 4-6 weeks, significant ranking improvements and traffic growth typically take 3-6 months of consistent optimization and content creation.

What's the minimum ad budget you recommend?

+

I recommend a minimum of $500/month in ad spend to generate meaningful data and results. However, the ideal budget depends on your industry, competition, and goals. We'll discuss the right budget for you during our consultation.

How long does a website project take?

+

A typical website project takes 2-4 weeks depending on complexity. Simple landing pages can be completed in a week, while full e-commerce sites may take 4-6 weeks. I'll provide a clear timeline during our initial discussion.

Do you work with businesses outside of India?

+

Absolutely! I work with clients worldwide. Digital marketing is location-independent, and I've successfully managed campaigns for businesses across multiple countries and time zones.

What makes you different from other digital marketers?

+

I don't just run campaigns — I build strategies. My approach is deeply data-driven, transparent, and results-focused. You'll always know exactly what I'm doing, why I'm doing it, and how it's performing. Plus, you get a complete marketing partner, not just a service provider.
